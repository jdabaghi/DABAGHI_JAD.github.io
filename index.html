Research interests

  • Variational inequalities:
    • Elliptic problems in contact mechanics, parabolic problems, hyperbolic problems in dynamic contact mechanics.
  • Numerical methods:
    • Finite elements, finite volumes, Discontinuous Galerkin methods, HHO methods.
  • Iterative solvers:
    • Semismooth Newton (Newton-min, Newton-Fischer-Burmeister, Newton-Mangasarian), GMRES, Multigrid, PCG.
  • A posteriori error estimate and adaptivity:
    • Residual a posteriori estimators, equilibrated flux a posteriori estimators, distinction of the error components, adaptive stopping criteria, efficiency.
  • Multiphase flow:
    • Storage of radioactive wastes, sustainable and renewable ressources, two-phase Darcy flows, phase transition, complementarity constraints.
  • Time parallelization:
    • Parareal-in-time algorithm for parabolic and hyperbolic problems.
  • Stochastic resolutions:
    • Monte-Carlo methods for Brownian motions and for the Boltzmann equation.
